# PolicyMesh benchmark results

Generated by `node benchmark/run-benchmark.mjs` against the built `dist/` CLI.
Corpus: labeled fixtures in `benchmark/labels.json`, each pointing at a repo
under `test/fixtures/`. Ground truth (intent, expected finding kinds, expected
rating) is fixed by fixture design — the runner scores the audit engine against
it. Benign cases include deliberate false-positive traps a naive detector would flag.
